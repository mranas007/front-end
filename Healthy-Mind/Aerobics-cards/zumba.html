Merengue

DESCRIPTION: Merengue, a basic Zumba move, involves easy-to-follow dance steps with a marching-like motion, emphasizing hip movements and maintaining a consistent rhythm.

CONDITIONS:
  • Focuses on rhythmic movements
  • Suitable for beginners
  • Low-impact dance routine
BENEFITS:
  • Enhances coordination
  • Promotes cardiovascular health
  • Boosts mood and energy levels

Salsa

DESCRIPTION: Salsa, a lively Zumba move, incorporates syncopated steps inspired by Latin dance, emphasizing hip swaying and quick footwork to a vibrant beat.

CONDITIONS:
  • Emphasizes hip movements
  • Requires coordination and agility
  • Moderate intensity dance style
BENEFITS:
  • Improves flexibility and balance
  • Enhances cardiovascular endurance
  • Tones lower body muscles

Cumbia

DESCRIPTION: Cumbia, a Zumba move inspired by Colombian folk dance, involves smooth, flowing movements combined with subtle hip sways.

CONDITIONS:
  • Focuses on fluid movements
  • Emphasizes grace and flow
  • Requires coordination and flexibility
BENEFITS:
  • Increases flexibility
  • Boosts endurance and stamina
  • Tones muscles throughout the body

Reggaeton

DESCRIPTION: Reggaeton, an energetic Zumba move, incorporates fast-paced movements influenced by hip-hop and Latin rhythms, involving body isolations, twists, and dynamic footwork.

CONDITIONS:
  • High-energy dance routine
  • Challenges coordination and agility
  • Requires quick and dynamic movements
BENEFITS:
  • Burns calories effectively
  • Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Enhances overall body coordination
