Guided Body Scan

DESCRIPTION: Guided Body Scan, involves following an instructor's voice, systematically moving attention through the body parts.

CONDITIONS:
  • Requires an audio guide or instructor
  • Promotes relaxation and body awareness
  • Helpful for beginners
BENEFITS:
  • Reduces stress and tension
  • Enhances relaxation response
  • Increases body-mind connection

Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR)

DESCRIPTION: Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR), involves systematically tensing and relaxing muscle groups, promoting relaxation and stress relief.

CONDITIONS:
  • Focuses on muscle tension release
  • Requires guidance or instructions
  • Suitable for stress and anxiety
BENEFITS:
  • Reduces muscle tension
  • Enhances relaxation and calmness
  • Alleviates stress and anxiety symptoms

Mindful Body Scan

DESCRIPTION: Mindful Body Scan, involves non-judgmentally observing bodily sensations, fostering mindfulness and present moment awareness.

CONDITIONS:
  • Focuses on mindfulness practice
  • Emphasizes observing sensations
  • Cultivates non-reactive awareness
BENEFITS:
  • Increases mindfulness skills
  • Enhances present moment awareness
  • Encourages acceptance and non-judgment

Breath-Centered Body Scan

DESCRIPTION: Breath-Centered Body Scan, focuses on observing body sensations while anchoring attention on the breath, enhancing relaxation and awareness.

CONDITIONS:
  • Integrates breath awareness
  • Emphasizes relaxation and body sensations
  • Enhances mindful breathing
BENEFITS:
  • Combines breath awareness and body scan
  • Enhances relaxation response
  • Increases mind-body connection
About
