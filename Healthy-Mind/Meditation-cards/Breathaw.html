Deep Breathing Exercise

DESCRIPTION: Deep Breathing Exercise, involves slow, deliberate breaths, inhaling deeply through the nose and exhaling slowly through the mouth.

CONDITIONS:
  • Emphasizes slow and intentional breaths
  • Utilizes diaphragmatic breathing
  • Supports relaxation and stress reduction
BENEFITS:
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Enhances relaxation response
  • Promotes mental clarity and focus

Counted Breathing

DESCRIPTION: Counted Breathing, involves inhaling for a specific count, holding briefly, and exhaling for the same count, focusing on rhythmic breathing.

CONDITIONS:
  • Focuses on rhythmic breathing patterns
  • Utilizes breath counting technique
  • Enhances focus and concentration
BENEFITS:
  • Regulates breathing patterns
  • Calms the mind and body
  • Improves attention and mindfulness

Box Breathing Technique

DESCRIPTION: Box Breathing Technique, involves inhaling, holding, exhaling, and holding the breath again, each for an equal count, creating a box-like pattern.

CONDITIONS:
  • Focuses on square breath pattern
  • Utilizes balanced breathing intervals
  • Promotes relaxation and focus
BENEFITS:
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Increases mental clarity and awareness
  • Promotes emotional balance

Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing)

DESCRIPTION: Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing), involves alternating breathing through each nostril, balancing energy and calming the mind.

CONDITIONS:
  • Utilizes alternate nostril breathing
  • Focuses on balancing energy flow
  • Supports relaxation and mental clarity
BENEFITS:
  • Balances nervous system
  • Enhances mental focus and relaxation
  • Regulates breath and energy flow
